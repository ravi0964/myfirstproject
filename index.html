Types of Visas

Business Visa

A business visa is a kind of movement authorisation that empowers an individual to go to one more nation to direct business there. They could lead business during the visit that isn't viewed as work or work.

Read More
Work Visa

A work visa is a document that enables you to live and work outside of your home country. To be eligible for a work visa, you must fulfil a number of requirements that differ depending on the nation you wish to work in.

Read More
Tourist Visa

With a tourist or travel visa, a person is only permitted to enter and remain in a foreign nation for short-term vacations and sightseeing. The holder of these visas is not allowed to work or conduct any business in the nation of the host.

Read More
Student Visa

A student visa is a type of official document that is given to students in order to aid their travel and educational pursuits, Student visas are usually issued for short-term purposes. To study abroad, you must have a student visa.
